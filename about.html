About Us

Our purpose in the creation of this website is to provide the general public information about matters surrounding animals, and more specifically, how animals can help your emotions as well as creating an interactive, engaging and interesting website about a well liked topic, animals.

Anthony Quattrocchi

Favorite animal: Sloths 4 years ago, Anthony came across an injured baby sloth lying in the middle of the road by his house. He decided it would be best to help that sloth recover so it can live a full and happy life. Today, that sloth is almost fully recovered and Anthony expects to release it back into the wild by the end of next year.
